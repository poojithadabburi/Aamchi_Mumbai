ISKCON

The ISKCON Temple, also known as the Hare Krishna Temple, is a prominent religious site located in the Juhu area of Mumbai, India. Here are some key points about the temple:

Features
Architecture: The temple showcases modern architecture with intricate carvings and beautiful domes. It features a large main shrine dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha. Spiritual Atmosphere: The temple is known for its serene environment, vibrant artwork, and devotional chants.

Location
ISKCON Temple, Juhu Street Address: Hare Krishna Land, Juhu, Mumbai, Maharashtra, 400049, India. Coordinates Approximate Coordinates: 19.1003° N latitude and 72.8258° E longitude.

Activities
Darshan: Visitors can participate in daily worship (puja) and view the deities during darshan. Cultural Programs: Regular spiritual discourses, kirtans (devotional singing), and festivals are held, attracting devotees and tourists alike. Vegetarian Restaurant: The temple houses a popular restaurant, offering a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes.

Surroundings
Nearby Attractions: Located near Juhu Beach, making it convenient for visitors to explore both the temple and the beach area.
Shopping: There are shops within the temple complex selling devotional items, books, and souvenirs.
