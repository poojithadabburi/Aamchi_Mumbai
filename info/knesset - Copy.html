Knesset Eliyahoo Synagogue

Knesset Eliyahoo is one of the oldest and most prominent synagogues in Mumbai, located in the Kala Ghoda area. It is known for its stunning architecture and rich history, serving as a significant place of worship for the Jewish community.

Location
Location: Kala Ghoda, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Type: Heritage synagogue

History
Established in 1884, Knesset Eliyahoo was built by the Bene Israel community, one of the oldest Jewish communities in India. The synagogue was named after the biblical prophet Elijah and has served as a vital center for the community's spiritual and cultural life.

Attractions
Architectural Beauty: The synagogue features stunning stained glass windows, intricate woodwork, and a grand bima (platform) for the Torah reading.
Cultural Events: Knesset Eliyahoo hosts various religious and cultural events, including festivals, which attract visitors from all backgrounds.

Features
Design: The synagogue is designed in a unique Indo-Saracenic style, showcasing elements of both Indian and Moorish architecture.
Community Hub: It serves not only as a place of worship but also as a cultural hub for the Jewish community and a point of interest for tourists.

Best Time to Visit
Visitors are welcome throughout the year, but attending during Jewish festivals or Shabbat services can provide deeper insights into the community's traditions and practices.

Knesset Eliyahoo Synagogue is a vital part of Mumbai's cultural heritage, representing the city's diverse religious landscape and history.
