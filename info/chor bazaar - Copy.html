Chor Bazaar

Chor Bazaar, also known as the "Thieves Market," is one of the most famous flea markets in Mumbai, known for its antique and vintage items.

Location
Location: Mangaldas Market, Grant Road, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Type: Flea market

History
Established in the 19th century, Chor Bazaar has a rich history of trading stolen goods, which contributed to its name. Today, it offers a wide range of products from antiques to Bollywood posters.

Attractions
Unique Antiques: Visitors can find rare items, from vintage furniture to collectibles.
Local Crafts: The market also features handmade goods and textiles, showcasing local craftsmanship.

Best Time to Visit
The market is open daily, but weekends are especially lively, drawing more visitors and vendors.

Chor Bazaar is a treasure trove for those looking to discover unique items and experience the bustling local market culture.
