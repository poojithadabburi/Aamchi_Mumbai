Versova Beach

Versova Beach is located in the western suburbs of Mumbai, India. It’s known for its serene atmosphere, contrasting with the more crowded beaches like Juhu. Here are some key points about Versova Beach:

Features
Scenic Views: Offers beautiful views of the Arabian Sea, especially during sunrise and sunset.
Less Crowded: Generally quieter than other beaches in Mumbai, making it a popular spot for those seeking a peaceful experience.
Fishing Village: Versova retains some of its fishing village charm, with local fishermen and their boats often seen along the shore.

Location
Coordinates: Approximately 18.9629° N latitude and 72.8265° E longitude.
Proximity: Khotachiwadi is located near the Girgaum Chowpatty and is easily accessible from other parts of Mumbai, being about 3 kilometers from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CST).

Activities

Walking and Jogging: The beach is great for early morning walks and jogs.
Photography: Popular among photographers for its picturesque setting.
Community Clean-ups: There have been initiatives to clean the beach, led by local volunteers and organizations.

Surroundings
Nearby Attractions: Close to other areas of interest, like the Versova Village and the bustling streets of Andheri.
Restaurants and Cafes: A variety of eateries nearby offer local and international cuisine.
